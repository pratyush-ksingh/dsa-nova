## Real-World Use Case
Epidemiological modeling at the WHO uses binary search on infection radius to determine containment zone boundaries that cover all reported cases. Traffic management systems at Waze identify red zones (congestion areas) by binary searching for the minimum radius that encompasses all incident reports in a region.
